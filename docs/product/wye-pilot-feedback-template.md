## Demo Script

1. Ask the participant to choose a River Wye section they know.
2. Ask what they would check before paddling it.
3. Ask them to inspect the map, hazards, access notes, gauge context, and seeded reports.
4. Ask them to add one real piece of local knowledge using the demo contribution flow.
5. Ask what would make them trust or distrust the information.
